About The Creator

Hello, my name is Autumn! I reside in Eastern Massachusetts. I use my hands to create art and graphic design. I'm working towards completion of my BFA while taking courses in graphic design, photography, and motion graphics. I believe that my best work is created from a combination of using my intuition, finding inspiration from the natural world around me, and crafting things by hand. My most desired work environments are those that surround me with other creatives, allow for the burning of sage and crystals galore, and are dog mom (and dog) friendly.
